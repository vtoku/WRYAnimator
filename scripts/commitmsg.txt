Add Definitions PropertyTemplates (FbxNode/FbxSkeleton/FbxAnimStack/...)

The FBX SDK resolves class-default properties from Definitions
PropertyTemplate blocks; Blender and assimp both write them. This was the
last structural difference vs Blender's MotionBuilder-accepted output.
Templates added for Model (full FbxNode set), NodeAttribute (FbxSkeleton),
AnimationStack, AnimationLayer, and AnimationCurveNode.

Round-trip verified incl. the blendshape path (104 tracks, 48 morphs).
